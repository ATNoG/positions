Why work with us?

ATNOG is the leading Portuguese research group in next generation system, offering a dynamic environment where different specialized areas address the challenges of the  next generation systems. This international environment is strongly engaged with national and international industry, and is highly respected in the academic community. ATNOG is a regular contributor to standardization bodies and to open-source software. The group offers a large flexibility for self-improvement, providing an ideal environment for pursuing academic studies.
